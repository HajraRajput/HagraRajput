About Me

I’m Hajra Rajput, a creative professional with 8+ years of experience in graphic design, video editing, content creation, and social media management. Over the years, I’ve worked with brands and clients across Pakistan, India, the United States, and the United Kingdom, delivering designs that not only look beautiful but also create impact.

From logos, flyers, and product packaging to illustrations, social media posts, and video editing, my expertise lies in crafting strategic and visually compelling content that aligns with brand identity and engages the right audience.

I’ve had the privilege of working with companies like Mariyal (India), Silverlight Research Group (UK), Bentalia Homes (US), Aksums Technologies, Nexrah Innovations, and more, helping them achieve a stronger digital presence.

With skills in Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Premiere Pro, XD, Figma, and Canva, I bring both creativity and technical expertise to every project. My goal is simple: to help individuals and businesses turn ideas into bold, beautiful, and memorable designs.
